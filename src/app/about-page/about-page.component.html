About Us

Vacation Destination is a tourism website. The users can take a survey to help them decide where they can go for their next vacation!

Users can explore videos and pictures on the possible locations from the survey.

At Pathfinder Travel, we developed this system to expose people to different locations around the world. Our website's purpose is to provide information on vacation destinations to facilitate making decisions on where to go for the next vacation. We also believe in sharing ideas, so our website also features a blog page where users will have the ability to share ideas and experiences on past vacations to help others!
